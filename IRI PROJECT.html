Welcome to Irrigation Research Institute

The Irrigation Research Institute (IRI), Roorkee was established to carry out research and development activities in the field of irrigation and water resources.

Our mandate includes conducting experimental and theoretical studies in hydraulics, hydrology, soil mechanics, and related disciplines to solve complex engineering problems.
